Missions

God's people are people on a mission. Just as God calls us in love to Himself, so we are called to others in love. We seek to love our neighbours as we love ourselves. In the Village Church this manifests itself in a number of ways:


  • •We are a church which serves a number of community organizations, from AA to the Boy Scouts and Girl Scouts meet in our building.
  • •We support our local food bank, providing food and gifts.
  • •We sponsor Mission trips to local (Worcester County) mission sites such as Mustard Seed, a soup kitchen for the needy.
  • •We support the Heifer Project and Living Waters mission for third world countries.
  • •We contribute to The United Church of Christ's Wider Mission and the Massachusetts Conference of the United Church of Christ.
  • •We participate in the Peace of Bread community meal offered weekly at the Presbyterian Church.
